Time Management in Object Oriented Design Interview

1) Requirement Gathering (3 - 5 min)
2) Use Cases (3 - 6 min)
3) Identify the Core classes (3 - 6 min)
4) Identify the fields of each class (5 - 10 min)
5) Identify the Relationship between the classes (5 - 10 min)
6) Identify the Actions of the classes (5 - 10 min)
7) Code (5 - 8 min)
8) Follow-up questions (3 - 4 min)
